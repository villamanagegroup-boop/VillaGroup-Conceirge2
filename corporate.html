Fast Placement Protocol

When an assignment is confirmed, we move. Most nurse housing placements are confirmed within 48–72 hours of the request — often faster for established agency partners.

Agency-Direct Billing

Invoices go directly to the staffing agency — not the nurse. Clean, itemized, and formatted for agency accounting. No financial friction for the clinician.

Consistent Quality Standards

Every unit meets our furnished housing standards — fully equipped kitchen, WiFi, linens, and utilities included. The same quality your nurses expect, every assignment.

Extension-Ready Leases

13-week leases with straightforward extension options when contracts are renewed. No renegotiation headaches when an assignment goes longer than expected.
