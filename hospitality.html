Choose what fits your family's needs and your placement's length. Every tier is available à la carte or bundled. All are available from day one.

Tier 01

Welcome & Arrival

Essential Welcome Kit — everyday basics for move-in day
Comfort Welcome Kit — candles, premium toiletries, local guide
Luxury Welcome Kit — fully curated, personalized arrivals
Branded Villa Concierge Co. tote bag with every placement

Every family receives a branded contact card so they always know how to reach us.

02
Tier 02

Fridge & Pantry Stocking

Basic Arrival Stock — essentials to get through the first 48 hours
Custom Family Stock — dietary preferences, kids' favorites, household specifics
Full Weekly Grocery Restocking — on a schedule, every week
Special dietary accommodations — vegan, allergen-free, kosher, and more

Stocking preferences are collected during our intake call so we get it right from day one.

All housekeeping staff are vetted, background-checked, and bonded.

Getting Started

Adding Arrive & Thrive™ Is Simple

It can be requested at placement, added mid-stay, or bundled into a standing agreement for ongoing partners.

01

Request at Intake

Tell us what you need when you call. Our team captures all family details — size, ages, pets, dietary needs, preferences — during the 24-hour intake.

02

We Coordinate Everything

We source, purchase, and prepare every tier selected before move-in. Nothing is left for the family to figure out on arrival day.

03

Ongoing Support Activates

Weekly check-ins, scheduled restocking, and housekeeping continue throughout the stay. We don't disappear after move-in.
