Our Story

Where We Come From

Villa Concierge Co. was founded on a simple belief: that families navigating displacement deserve more than a transaction. They deserve a team that shows up, anticipates their needs, and makes an impossible situation manageable.

We watched families receive housing that technically met policy requirements but failed them as human beings — sterile units, empty fridges, no one to call when something broke, and no acknowledgment that the family inside was going through one of the hardest experiences of their lives. We knew we could do better. So we built something that does.

"We built Villa Concierge Co. to bridge the gap between the insurance industry's need for reliable housing partners and families' need for genuine human support."

We built Villa Concierge Co. under the umbrella of Villa Management Group to bridge the gap between the insurance industry's need for reliable housing partners and families' need for genuine human support. The result is a company that speaks fluently to carriers, adjusters, and HR departments — while never losing sight of the family at the center of every placement.

From our three-channel housing network to our Arrive & Thrive™ hospitality program and our family support services, every part of how we operate was designed to make the hardest days a little softer. That belief hasn't changed since the day we opened our doors — and it's the reason our clients come back, and our families remember us long after they've returned home.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Four principles that guide every placement, every invoice, and every interaction — with families and with partners.

01

Speed With Care

Fast placement doesn't mean rushed. We move quickly and we get it right. Families are placed in hours because we've built the systems and the network to make that possible — not because we're cutting corners on fit or quality.

02

Dignity Always

Every family, regardless of coverage level, receives the same level of respect and attention. A basic ALE policy and a premium policy both come with a team that answers the phone, follows up, and treats the family like people — not a case number.

03

Transparency

Clean invoicing, honest communication, and documented everything. We never surprise our partners. If there's an issue, we call first. If there's a cost, it's on the invoice. If we can't do something, we say so — and then we find someone who can.

04

Whole-Family Thinking

Housing is the start. We stay engaged until the family is truly settled — school enrollment navigated, pets comfortable, kids unpacked, and the household running. We don't disappear after move-in. That's the easy part.

Our Parent Company

A Villa Management Group Company

Villa Concierge Co. is proudly operated under the umbrella of Villa Management Group — a property management and hospitality organization with deep experience in residential housing, furnished rentals, and guest services across the Southeast.

Villa Management Group provides Villa Concierge Co. with access to a controlled inventory of managed properties, established vendor relationships, and the operational infrastructure to serve families and partners at scale. The two companies share values, but Villa Concierge Co. was purpose-built for the relocation and displacement market — with its own team, its own processes, and its own standard of care.
